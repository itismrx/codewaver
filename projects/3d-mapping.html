What It Does

LiDAR and photogrammetry into clean, web-ready 3D models

Processes raw LiDAR and photogrammetry data into clean, web-ready 3D models. Built for municipalities, infrastructure firms, and engineering consultancies that need spatial intelligence without specialised GIS software.

  • Browser-based 3D visualisation
  • LiDAR + photogrammetry input
  • Export to GIS formats

Point Cloud Processing

Cleans, classifies, and meshes raw scan data from LiDAR or photogrammetry sources.

Web 3D Viewer

Share interactive models via URL — no plugins, no installs, any browser.

Measurement Tools

Distance, area, and volume measurements directly in the browser viewer.

Data Export

Export to GeoJSON, OBJ, IFC, and DXF for downstream GIS or CAD workflows.
