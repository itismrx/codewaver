Find Lawyers

Connecting people with the right verified lawyer — in seconds, not days.

iOS & Android Verified profiles Search by specialisation & location
What It Does

Removes friction from finding legal help

A mobile-first platform that removes the friction from finding legal help. Users browse verified lawyer profiles filtered by practice area, city, language, and rating. Lawyers manage their availability and receive direct enquiries through the app.

  • Instant profile discovery
  • Lawyer verification system
  • In-app enquiry and scheduling

Smart Search

Filter by practice area, city, language, and budget to find the right match.

Verified Profiles

Licensed credentials are checked and displayed on every lawyer profile.

Rating System

Client reviews with moderation to ensure quality and authenticity.

Direct Enquiry

Message a lawyer directly from their profile or book a consultation.

How It Works

Legal help in three steps

1

Search by Need & Location

Describe your legal issue and set your city or postcode to surface nearby specialists.

2

Browse Verified Profiles

Compare verified credentials, client ratings, languages spoken, and availability.

3

Send Enquiry or Book

Message a lawyer directly or book a consultation slot — all within the app.
