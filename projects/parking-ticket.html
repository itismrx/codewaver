Parking Ticket Management

Digital enforcement for municipalities — from ticket issuance to payment and dispute resolution.

Mobile issuance Online payment Dispute management portal
What It Does

Replaces paper-based enforcement with a fully digital workflow

Officers issue tickets on mobile devices, citizens pay online, and disputes are managed through a self-service portal — reducing administrative overhead for councils and improving the citizen experience.

  • Offline-capable mobile app for officers
  • Citizen payment portal
  • Council analytics dashboard

Mobile Issuance

Officers photograph and issue tickets on-site with an offline-capable mobile app.

Online Payment

Citizens pay fines via card or UPI — no queues, no paper.

Dispute Portal

Citizens submit evidence and track resolution status through a self-service portal.

Analytics Dashboard

Enforcement patterns, revenue reports, and hotspot maps for council teams.

How It Works

Issue to resolution, fully digital

1

Officer Issues Ticket

Officer photographs the vehicle and issues a digital ticket on the mobile app — offline or online.

2

Citizen Receives Notification

An SMS and email notification is sent with the fine details and a payment link.

3

Pay or Dispute Online

Citizens pay via card or UPI, or raise a dispute through the self-service portal.
